MyMediTalessupports provider organizations in meeting the 21st Century Cures Act's Interoperability and Patient Access Final Rule so healthcare professionals can focus less on routing information and more on patient care.

How to get started

Follow these steps below and fill out our form to loop us in

Step 1
Log a Service Request

Submit a ticket in your EHR client portal to enable MyMediTalesfor Patient Access

Step 2
Get your Conformance statement

In the client portal retrieve your FHIR conformance statement

http://yourEHRvendor/fhir/DSTU2/conformancestatement/metadata

http://yourEHRvendor/fhir/R4/conformancestatement/metadata

Step 3
Share your details with MyMediTales

Fill out our form or email us at hello@MyMediTales.com
